The MAMS YMBC Carnival

The MAMS YMBC Carnival, jointly hosted by MAMS and YMBC in Kothamangalam, stands as South India's premier campus festival. This high-energy annual event unites colleges for intense intercollegiate competitions across diverse categories, including major sports, cultural performances like the Groove Gala, and management contests.

More than just a competition, the carnival is a vibrant platform that fosters personal development, teamwork, and cultural exchange.
